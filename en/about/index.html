Research & Study

Research

Research Agents & Executable Reasoning

A manuscript describing IDAgent is in preparation. The system connects research ideas with datasets through retrieval, adaptation, and reflection. GeoSketch-CoT turns geometry reasoning steps into editable SVG scratchpads. IDAgent is presented here as work in progress, not as a publication.
