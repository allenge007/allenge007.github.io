Problem

Ideas describe goals and hypotheses while datasets describe fields, modalities, and empirical boundaries; keyword search cannot reliably align the two.

02

Approach

We constructed a large aligned corpus, trained an ID-SBERT/Faiss retriever, designed semantic clustering and cold-start evaluation, and implemented a RARO generation workflow.
